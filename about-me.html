Born in Dublin, Ireland, I have always had a strong sense of pride about my heritage. I love learning about my Irish history and when I visit my homeland, I do lots of visitations of where my ancestors lived. I've even seen some of the old houses where my great-great grandparents grew up. I come from both the northernmost and southernmost counties in Ireland, being Donegal and Kerry, in that order. Having visited almost every other year pre-covid, I have a strong connection to the sea, the beach, and the Wicklow mountains, along with my sizeable extended family.
